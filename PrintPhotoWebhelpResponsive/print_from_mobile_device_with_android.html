Print from mobile device with Android

  1. Go to the Google Play Store and confirm if the HP Print Service Plugin is installed. Install the plugin if it is not installed.

  2. Enable Plugin.

    New install: Tap notification to enable.

    Previously installed: Go to Printing settings in your mobile settings to enable.

  3. Toggle on HP Print Service Plugin and your mobile device is now enabled for mobile printing!
