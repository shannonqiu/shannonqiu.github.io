Print from mobile device (iOS) with AirPrint™

Printing using Apple’sAirPrint is supportedforiOS 4.2 and Mac OS X 10.7 or later.

To use AirPrint,ensure the following:

  1. Make sure your device and printer are connect to the same network as yourAirPrintenableddevice.
  2. Open the page or photo you want to print and tap the 'Share' button.

  3. Swipe screen to reveal print button on bottom row, then tap the print icon.

  4. Confirm the correct printer and print setting are selected. Tap 'Print' and collect your pages!
